# Project 4 — Vendor Risk Assessment

**Organization:** White Plains Hospital (simulated)
**Vendor Assessed:** MedBill Pro Solutions LLC (fictional)
**Prepared By:** Nadhir Choudhury — GRC Analyst
**Framework:** HIPAA | NIST CSF | SOC 2 | TPRM Best Practices
**Tool:** Microsoft Excel
**Date:** April 2026

---

## What This Project Covers

A complete third-party vendor risk assessment for a fictional
healthcare IT vendor providing EHR billing and revenue cycle
management services to White Plains Hospital.

Evaluates vendor security across 7 risk categories using
a structured 36-question security questionnaire.

---

## What Is Inside the File

- Cover page with vendor profile and assessment result
- 36-question security questionnaire with vendor responses
- Risk scoring across 7 categories
- Gap identification and assessor notes
- Formal risk scoring summary with overall recommendation
- Formal written assessment report
- Remediation conditions and sign-off page

---

## Risk Categories Assessed

- Organizational Security
- Data Protection and Privacy
- Access Control and Identity Management
- Network and Infrastructure Security
- Compliance and Certifications
- Physical Security
- Vendor and Contract Management

---

## Assessment Result

Vendor scored 95 out of 100.
Result — APPROVED with conditions.
Two gaps identified requiring remediation within 90 days.
