I am a former Surgical Technologist with hands-on experience at **White Plains Hospital** and **Mount Sinai Health System**, now transitioning into cybersecurity — specifically **Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC)**.

After years of working in one of the most regulated environments that exists — following HIPAA protocols daily, maintaining audit-ready documentation, preparing for Joint Commission inspections, and managing risk in high-stakes surgical settings — I realized something important:

**I was already thinking like a GRC professional. I just did not have the title yet.**

This portfolio is the formal proof of that thinking. Every project here was built to mirror how GRC work is actually done inside real organizations — using real frameworks, real deliverable formats, and real decision-making logic applied to a simulated acute care hospital environment.
